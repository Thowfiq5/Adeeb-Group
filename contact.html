    Frequently Asked Questions

    Quick answers to common questions about our services and processes

    What areas do you serve?

    We serve clients nationwide with our facility management and IT services. Our main offices are in New York, Chicago, and San Francisco, but we have teams that can deploy to locations across the United States.

    How quickly can you start a project?

    For most projects, we can begin within 1-2 weeks after contract signing. Emergency services can often be deployed within 24-48 hours. The exact timeline depends on project complexity and scope.

    Do you offer 24/7 support?

    Yes, we offer 24/7 support for both facility management emergencies and critical IT issues. Our support team is always available to address urgent matters and ensure minimal disruption to your operations.

    Can you handle both FM and IT services together?

    Absolutely! Our integrated approach is one of our key strengths. We can manage both your physical facility and digital infrastructure, ensuring seamless coordination between both areas for optimal operational efficiency.

    What industries do you specialize in?

    We serve a wide range of industries including corporate offices, manufacturing, healthcare, education, retail, and technology. Our solutions are tailored to meet the specific needs and compliance requirements of each industry.
