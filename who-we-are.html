About ADEEB GROUP

Adeeb group is a leading IMS-certified Organization with ISO 9001, 14001, 41001, and ISO 45001, ISO 41001 (Facility Management), ISO 50001 (Energy Management), OSHAD, and TQM (Total Quality Management). Established in 1994 and headquartered in Abu Dhabi, we deliver integrated facility management and MEP contracting services across multiple sectors in the UAE and Seychelles.

With decades of experience, we provide tailored solutions for residential, commercial and industrial clients. Our teams of engineers and technicians focus on quality, safety, and sustainable outcomes that reduce costs and improve asset performance.

Our Check Green initiative promotes eco-friendly methods, chemical-free cleaning, improved indoor air quality, and responsible waste management to reduce environmental impact while controlling costs.

We partner with strategic consultants to develop energy-efficient measures and alternative energy solutions. From audits to implementation and operations, our systems integrate seamlessly into clients' core operations to deliver measurable results.
